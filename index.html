About Homeopathy

WHAT IS HOMOEOPATHY ?

HOMOEOPATHY is a 223 year old system of medicine which was discovered by Dr. Christian Fredrich Samuel Hahnemann, in 1796 in Germany, which has now flourished as a mainstream medicine in our era. He invented this system on the doctrine of LIKE CURES LIKE (or कट्याने काटा काढणे or लोहा लोहे को मारता है). When Dr. Hahnemann started the practice of homoeopathy, it was not even considered medicine and he was laughed at and criticized about the same. But he was adamant and determined to introduce the world with the best from of medicine which had no side effects, which was natural and which was permanent. Slowly he and his disciples spread about the system and it was worldwide.

Thus homoeopathy is famous for removing the disease or ailment in its whole extent.
